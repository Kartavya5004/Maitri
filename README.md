# 🌌 Overview

**MAITRI** is an advanced AI-powered astronaut assistance system designed to support both the **mental** and **physical well-being** of astronauts during long-duration space missions.

The platform combines:
- 🎙️ Voice interaction
- 😊 Emotion recognition
- 🧠 Cognitive wellness support
- 📊 Health monitoring assistance
- 🤖 Conversational AI

to create a smart onboard companion capable of improving astronaut resilience, emotional stability, and mission performance.

---

# 🧩 Problem Statement

Astronauts experience multiple physical and psychological challenges in space environments.

## Physical Challenges
- Bone density loss
- Cardiovascular deconditioning
- Visual impairment
- Radiation exposure
- Sleep cycle disruption

## Psychological Challenges
- Isolation & confinement
- Cognitive fatigue
- Emotional stress
- Reintegration stress
- Loneliness during long missions

These issues can reduce efficiency, increase mission risk, and negatively affect long-term astronaut health.

---

# 💡 Proposed Solution

MAITRI acts as a real-time AI companion capable of:

✅ Emotion Detection  
✅ Conversational Assistance  
✅ Facial Expression Interpretation  
✅ Voice-Based Stress Analysis  
✅ Wellness Monitoring  
✅ Adaptive Recommendations  

The system uses multimodal AI pipelines to analyze:
- Facial behavior
- Voice tone
- Emotional patterns
- Conversational responses

and provide personalized astronaut support.
